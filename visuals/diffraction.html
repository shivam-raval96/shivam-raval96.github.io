What you're seeing

Each row of digits has a width equal to the diffraction intensity at that height. Bright fringes → wide lines. Dark fringes → no text.

The digit stream is the decimal expansion of π, flowing continuously through the lit regions only.

More slits → sharper, brighter principal maxima with weaker secondary peaks — exactly like a diffraction grating.
